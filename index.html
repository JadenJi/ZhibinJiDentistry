Dr. Zhibin Ji, a renowned oral surgeon trained in head and neck oncology in Nanjing, China, embarked on a journey to America in 1995, seeking a freer life. From humble beginnings — bussing trays as a waiter and sleeping on garage floors — he tirelessly studied after work, eventually earning his ADA certification to practice dentistry in the U.S. His extensive expertise in oral surgery now translates into exceptional dental care, making Dr. Ji a skilled and compassionate provider.

After 20 years in Redding, Dr. Ji proudly calls America his home. His clinic is located in Shasta Lake, which you can find here. With a commitment to providing top-quality dental care, Dr. Ji combines decades of expertise with a compassionate approach. Visit his clinic in Shasta Lake and experience the difference in care firsthand.
